ABOUT

Andaman is the westernmost region of India and arguably one of the most interesting places to visit on the island.

WHEN TO VISIT ANDAMAN

Summers on the island is dry and extremely hot
Spring remains an ideal time to travel to Andaman.

HOW TO GET TO ANDAMAN
By plane

You can take a direct flight from Kolkata, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Delhi and Vizag to reach Andaman within 2.5 to 3.5 hours!

By boat

Board a ship from Kolkata and Chennai 3-4 times a month and only once from Visakhapatnam. However, the travel time would be around 3-4 days (50-60 hours).
